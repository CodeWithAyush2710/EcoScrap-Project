About EcoScrap Network

Connecting metal scrap locations with buyers for a sustainable future

Our Mission

At EcoScrap Network, our mission is to create a circular economy by connecting scrap metal sources with those who can reuse them, promoting large-scale sustainability efforts in the metal industry.

Our Vision

We envision a world where metal resources are fully reused, drastically reducing waste and contributing to a cleaner, greener planet.

Our Journey

2022 - The Beginning

EcoScrap Network was founded with the idea to reduce metal waste and promote sustainable practices by leveraging digital technology to connect buyers and sellers.

2023 - Expanding Impact

We launched our platform, gaining traction with scrap metal providers and industries looking for sustainable metal reuse solutions.

2024 - Going Global

EcoScrap Network expanded internationally, partnering with organizations worldwide to scale metal recycling and reuse efforts.
